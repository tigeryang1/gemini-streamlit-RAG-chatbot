The Gemini Streamlit support assistant is designed for internal product help.

Supported capabilities:
- Answer questions about product setup, account onboarding, and deployment basics.
- Summarize known limitations and escalation paths.
- Reference internal runbooks when they are loaded into the knowledge base.

Known limitations:
- The sample app does not execute external tools.
- It should not invent account-specific production facts.
- If the answer is missing from retrieved context, it should say so explicitly.
