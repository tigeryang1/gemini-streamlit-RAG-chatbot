Support policy for the sample chatbot:

Severity levels:
- Sev 1: Production outage or complete service unavailability.
- Sev 2: Major feature degradation with business impact.
- Sev 3: Minor bug or workaround available.

Escalation guidance:
- Sev 1 issues should be escalated immediately to the on-call team.
- Billing-only questions should be routed to the billing queue.
- Feature requests should be recorded as product feedback, not incident tickets.

Response style:
- Confirm what evidence is in the knowledge base.
- Do not claim ticket status unless it appears in the retrieved documents.
